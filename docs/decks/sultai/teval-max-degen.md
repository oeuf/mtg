Why this is “max degen” in practice (without Field/Chasm/Crop)

You drown the table in cards (Rhystic + your draw/filter package).
You churn the graveyard (Orb/Crab/Supplier/Lich/Loam) and recur lands to keep Teval attacks online.
You cash out with either:
Rift → untap → lethal board, or
Living Death with Konrad/Jarad/Lord math.

Graveyard hate plan (still degenerate, still durable)

Kill the hate: Trophy / Grip / Boseiju, plus Muldrotha-style recursion isn’t available here, so we run higher density of “must-answer” interaction.
Insurance: Soul-Guide Lantern + Endurance let you fight other yards and sometimes blunt exile timing.
