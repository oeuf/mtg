## How this Teval list actually wins in tuned Bracket 3

* **Teval pressure loop:** attack → mill → recur land → churn recursion spells → Teval makes tokens when cards leave the graveyard. ([Gatherer][2])
* **“One big turn” closes:** **Cyclonic Rift** end step → untap → swing wide (tokens + Scute Swarm + Field of the Dead) and/or cast **Living Death** with protection. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
* **Graveyard-to-board damage:** **Syr Konrad** + big grave turns (Living Death / Victimize / repeated recursion).

# GC swaps / alternates (3-at-a-time packages) and what changes

All cards below are explicitly on the GC list. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])

### Option A — **Toolbox + staying power** (more consistent, less “Rift ends it”)

**IN:** Survival of the Fittest + The One Ring + Force of Will ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
**OUT:** Rhystic Study + Cyclonic Rift + Fierce Guardianship
**Effect:** You become **more proactive** at assembling the right creature lines and **more resilient** through attrition, but you lose the cleanest “reset/close” lever (Rift).

### Option B — **Turbo Teval** (fastest starts; closest to Bracket 4 feel)

**IN:** Ancient Tomb + Mana Vault + Chrome Mox ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
**OUT:** Rhystic Study + Cyclonic Rift + Fierce Guardianship
**Effect:** Much faster Teval / faster Living Death turns. **Downside:** many tuned pods will experience this as “nearly optimized,” even if still Bracket-3-legal on paper.

### Option C — **Tutor/disruption package** (meaner, more surgical)

**IN:** Demonic Tutor + Vampiric Tutor + Opposition Agent ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
**OUT:** Rhystic Study + Cyclonic Rift + Fierce Guardianship
**Effect:** You trade “draw + reset + free protect” for **precision + punishment**. Strong, but it pushes table experience toward “gotcha” and tends to generate salt.

**Strong warning (Bracket 3 integrity):**
Even though **Thassa’s Oracle** is also a GC, building around **Oracle + Demonic Consultation / Tainted Pact** tends to violate the “no early two-card combos” intent for Bracket 3. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
