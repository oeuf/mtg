Icetill Explorer

I should’ve highlighted it because it’s basically “mini-Crucible + extra land drop + self-mill” on a body, which is exactly what Teval/Field/Chasm wants.

If you’re running the Field/Chasm/Crop Rotation GC package, I’d swap it in over Satyr Wayfinder (same role, but Explorer is higher ceiling and keeps the engine going every turn).

Craterhoof Behemoth

Also yes: with Field of the Dead and Scute Swarm, Craterhoof is one of the cleanest “end the game now” closers available.

In that Teval build, I’d swap Craterhoof in over Massacre Wurm (unless your pod is token-swarm heavy), because Hoof converts your inevitability into immediate lethal more reliably.
