## Why this is “highest power Bracket 3” for a tuned pod

* **Rhystic Study** supplies the card volume you need to both develop Teval and keep interacting. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
* **Cyclonic Rift** is your cleanest “convert advantage → win” closer. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
* **Fierce Guardianship** protects the critical swing turn (Teval attack turn, Living Death, or Rift) without tempo loss. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])
* Everything else is tuned toward Teval’s engine: self-mill + land recursion + “cards leaving graveyard” churn → bodies → lethal board over time. ([Gatherer][2])

# GC swap options (3-card swaps) and what they change

All of the following cards are on the official GC list (updated list shown in the Oct 21, 2025 update). ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][3])

### Option 1: **“Toolbox + resilience”** (more consistent, less blowout)

Swap **IN**:

* Survival of the Fittest (GC)
* The One Ring (GC)
* Force of Will (GC)

Swap **OUT**:

* Rhystic Study, Cyclonic Rift, Fierce Guardianship

**Effect:** you become **more proactive and consistent** (find the right creature + sustain through hate), but you lose the “Rift ends the game” lever. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])

### Option 2: **“Turbo Teval”** (fastest starts; feels closest to Bracket 4)

Swap **IN**:

* Ancient Tomb (GC)
* Mana Vault (GC)
* Chrome Mox (GC)

Swap **OUT**:

* Rhystic Study, Cyclonic Rift, Fierce Guardianship

**Effect:** your deck becomes **much faster** at deploying Teval and casting Living Death-level plays, but many tuned pods will experience this as “near-Optimized.” ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][1])

### Option 3: **“Black disruption package”** (meaner, more punishing)

Swap **IN**:

* Demonic Tutor (GC)
* Opposition Agent (GC)
* Orcish Bowmasters (GC)

Swap **OUT**:

* Rhystic Study, Cyclonic Rift, Fierce Guardianship

**Effect:** you trade the blue “safety + reset” plan for **precision + punishment**. Strong in tuned metas, but it’s the package most likely to create salty games. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][3])

**My recommendation for your stated goal (“highest power tuned Bracket 3”):** stick with **Rhystic + Rift + Fierce** (current build). It maximizes win conversion in tuned pods while still playing “real” Bracket 3 games. ([MAGIC: THE GATHERING][3])
