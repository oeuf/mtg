## Shopping Strategy Guide

### Priority Tiers

1. **TIER 1: Universal Staples** - Cards used in all 4 Ext decks (buy first for maximum versatility)
2. **TIER 2: High-Versatility** - Cards used in 3+ Ext decks (excellent value)
3. **TIER 3: Reserved List Bombs** - Expensive, archetype-defining cards (buy when committed)
4. **TIER 4: Archetype-Specific** - Cards for single decks (buy when building that specific deck)

### Budget Recommendations

- **Budget <$500**: Focus on Tier 1 + Tier 2 (universal staples and multi-deck cards)
- **Budget $500-$1500**: Add selected Tier 4 cards for your primary archetype
- **Budget >$1500**: Consider Tier 3 Reserved List cards (Gaea's Cradle, Grim Monolith)

## Card Condition & Printing Notes

### Reserved List Cards (Will Never Be Reprinted)
- Gaea's Cradle
- Grim Monolith

These should be purchased in the best condition you can afford, as they're long-term holds.

### High Reprint Risk (May Drop in Price)
- Rhystic Study (already reprinted multiple times)
- Cyclonic Rift (reprinted in multiple sets)
- Force of Negation (Modern Horizons reprint possible)

Consider waiting for reprints or buying budget printings.

### Stable Investments
- Vampiric Tutor
- Demonic Tutor
- Crucible of Worlds
- Exploration

These have maintained value despite reprints.
